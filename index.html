About Sadbhawana Pathology Clinic

Sadbhawana Pathology Clinic is a leading diagnostic center located in the bustling city of Lucknow. Our clinic offers a wide range of pathology services, including blood tests, hemoglobin tests, total liquescence tests, and liver function tests, among others. We are dedicated to providing accurate and reliable test results to our patients, and our state-of-the-art laboratory equipment ensures the highest level of precision and efficiency.

Our team of experienced and qualified pathologists and technicians work tirelessly to deliver top-notch service to our patients. We understand the importance of timely and accurate diagnosis, which is why we offer prompt and efficient testing services. At Sadbhawana Pathology Clinic, we prioritize patient satisfaction, and our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always ready to assist you with any queries you may have.

We adhere to strict quality control measures to ensure that our test results are trustworthy and accurate. We take pride in our commitment to excellence and strive to deliver the best possible healthcare experience to our patients. Visit us today for all your pathology needs.
